About Image Guy

Our mission is to provide a fast, free, and easy-to-use tool for everyone to optimize their images for the web. We believe in a faster, more efficient, and greener internet.

Why Optimize?

Every image you optimize doesn't just improve your website's loading speed; it also reduces the energy required for data transfer and storage. This means a smaller digital footprint and a small but meaningful contribution to a more sustainable planet.

Our Technology

Image Guy is powered by the high-performance `sharp` library, running on a scalable serverless architecture. This allows us to offer top-tier optimization for various formats like JPEG, PNG, WebP, and AVIF without compromising on quality.
