Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2025

Image Handling

Images you upload are sent to our secure cloud servers (Amazon S3 and Netlify Functions) for the sole purpose of optimization. Original and optimized images may be stored temporarily to ensure service delivery and are automatically deleted. We do not access, share, or use your images for any other purpose.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. You are not required to create an account or provide any personal data to use Image Guy. We may use privacy-friendly analytics to monitor website traffic, but this data is anonymized and does not track individual users.

Third-Party Services

Our service is built upon professional cloud infrastructure provided by Netlify and Amazon Web Services (AWS). These services have their own robust privacy and security policies.
